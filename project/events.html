Canteen Class

The Canteen Class is a monthly series of sessions where passionate hospitality students can interact with industry professionals to get a real understanding of what it takes to be a part of the industry. Started in 2017, The Bombay Canteen has conducted 40+ Canteen Classes with over 1200 hospitality students!
